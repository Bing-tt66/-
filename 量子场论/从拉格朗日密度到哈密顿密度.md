书中给定的拉格朗日密度：
$$
\mathcal{L}=-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi\partial^\mu\varphi\partial_\mu\varphi-\frac{1}{2}Z_mm^2\varphi^2+\frac{1}{6}Z_gg\varphi^3+Y\varphi
$$
展开导数项
$$
\partial^\mu\varphi\partial_\mu\varphi=(\partial^0\varphi)(\partial_0\varphi)+(\partial^i\varphi)(\partial_i\varphi)=\dot{\varphi}^2-(\nabla\varphi)^2
$$
其中
- $\dot{\varphi}=\partial_0\varphi$是时间导数
- $\nabla\varphi$是空间导数
拉格朗日密度变成：
$$
\mathcal{L}=-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi[\dot{\varphi}^2-(\nabla\varphi)^2]-\frac{1}{2}Z_mm^2\varphi^2+\frac{1}{6}Z_gg\varphi^3+Y\varphi
$$
正则动量定义为：
$$
\Pi=\frac{\partial\mathcal{L}}{\partial\dot{\varphi}}
$$
$$
\frac{\partial\mathcal{L}}{\partial\dot{\varphi}}=\frac{\partial}{\partial\dot{\varphi}}[-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi\dot{\varphi}^2-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi(\nabla\varphi)^2-\frac{1}{2}Z_mm^2\varphi^2+\frac{1}{6}Z_gg\varphi^3+Y\varphi]=-Z_\varphi\dot{\varphi}
$$
所以
$$
\Pi=-Z_\varphi\dot{\varphi}\to\dot{\varphi}=-\frac{\Pi}{Z_\varphi}
$$
哈密顿量密度公式
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathcal{H}&=\Pi\dot{\varphi}-\mathcal{L}\\
&=\Pi(-\frac{\Pi}{Z_\varphi})-[-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi\dot{\varphi}^2-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi(\nabla\varphi)^2-\frac{1}{2}Z_mm^2\varphi^2+\frac{1}{6}Z_gg\varphi^3+Y\varphi]\\
&=-\frac{\Pi^2}{Z_\varphi}+\frac{1}{2}\frac{\Pi^2}{Z_\varphi}-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi(\nabla\varphi)^2+\frac{1}{2}Z_mm^2\varphi^2-\frac{1}{6}Z_gg\varphi^3-Y\varphi\\
&=-\frac{1}{2}\frac{\Pi^2}{Z_\varphi}-\frac{1}{2}Z_\varphi(\nabla\varphi)^2+\frac{1}{2}Z_mm^2
\varphi^3-\frac{1}{6}Z_gg\varphi^3-Y\varphi
\end{aligned}
$$
稳定场可忽略梯度项
$$
\mathcal{H}=-\frac{1}{2}\frac{\Pi^2}{Z_\varphi}+\frac{1}{2}Z_mm^2
\varphi^3-\frac{1}{6}Z_gg\varphi^3-Y\varphi
$$
